Menu Name
Mee Kung Fu
Summary

KungFu noodle same like hong kong noodles.the noodle (bihun/kuey teow/yellow mee) is wrapped with the egg and fried to make it crunchy. The gravy is cook separately and mixed various type of vegetable and it is very suitable menu for vegeterian.

Ingredients

For Noodle

  • 500g flat rice noodle/bihun/yellow noodles
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp thick Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

    Beat egg, heat oil in the pan. Add eggs, noodle and soy sauce, fried until crisp, reserved.

    For Gravy

  • 3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 150 g prawn
  • 100 g chicken meat, stripes
  • 2 bowl mix veges (bak choy/chinese cabbage/baby corn/carrot)
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp (or more) light soy sauce
  • 1 tsp white pepper
  • Salt if needed
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp corn flour*
  • 1/2 cup water*
  • 2 white eggs, beaten lightly

    • Direction

  • 1.Heat oil over medium heat.

  • 2.Add crushed garlics and fry until golden, add chicken and prawn.

  • 3.Add oyster sauce, light soy sauce, pepper and chicken stock, let it simmer for few minutes. Add vegetables and *corn flour mixture.

  • 4.Pour in the beaten white eggs slowly until the broth turn thick. Season well.

    To serve:

  • Place the noodle in the serving plate and pour the broth on top. Serve hot with pickled chili
