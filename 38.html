Menu Name
Baked Cheese Fish
Summary

the fish slices are quickly browned and then scrambled into smaller pieces with a flat spatula. Slices of cheese are then placed over the fish, letting it melt, and then the roll is placed on top of the cheese
Ingredients


Ingredients:

  • lb fish fillet (I always use haddock)
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2onion, thinly sliced (I slice into rings and break them apart and in half if they are too big)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3/4 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • salt & pepper

    • Direction


    Methods:

  • 1.Place fish in a greased pan with dabs of butter on top.

  • 2.Season with salt& pepper to taste. (this is where I sprinkle on lemon juice or lemon pepper if using) Cover with sliced onion.

  • 3.Pour on milk.

  • 4.Bake at 400 degrees for 12-15 minutes.

  • 5.Remove from oven and sprinkle with grated cheddar cheese and a little parsley if desired.

  • 6.Return to oven until cheese melts.
