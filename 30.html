Menu Name
Oriental Chicken Chop
Summary

The term "Chicken Maryland" or "Maryland Chicken" refers to a historic dish associated with the U.S. state of Maryland, but has other meanings from other nations. In its home base, the food dish consists of fried chicken served with a cream gravy
Ingredients


Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken marylands (approx 700g)

    • Marinade:
  • 1/2 tbsp oil
  • 1 1/2 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 2 tsp kecap manis (or thick caramel soy)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • Dash of pepper

    • Blend together finely:
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1/2 tbsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp ground turmeric

    • Direction


    Methods:

  • 1.Combine chicken with marinade ingredients. Rub the blended ingredients all over the chicken. Leave in the fridge to marinade for 3-4 hours or overnight.

  • 2.Preheat oven to 200C. Place chicken in a baking dish, skin side up, and roast for 50 minutes or until cooked. Halfway through cooking, use a brush to baste the chicken with the juices in the baking dish to keep it moist. Serve with steamed rice.
