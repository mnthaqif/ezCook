Menu Name
Mango Finger Crepe
Summary

Crepes are made by pouring a wheat batter onto a frying pan or flat circular hot plate, often with a trace of butter or vegetable oil on the pan's surface. The batter is spread evenly over the cooking surface of the pan or plate either by tilting the pan or by distributing the batter with an offset spatula or trowel. The consistency of the batter cannot be too thick, nor can the pan be too hot
Ingredients


Ingredients:

  • 425g can mango slices in syrup
  • 1 cup thickened cream
  • 1 tablespoon icing sugar mixture
  • 8 ready-made fresh crepes
  • Icing sugar mixture, to serve
    • Direction


    Method:

  • 1.Drain mangoes, reserving 1 tablespoon syrup. Using an electric mixer, beat cream and sugar until soft peaks form.

  • 2.Place 1 crepe on a flat surface. Brush crepe with a little reserved mango syrup. Spoon 2 tablespoons cream mixture 8cm in from edge closest to you. Top with 1 mango slice. Fold in sides and roll up to enclose filling. Place on a plate. Repeat with remaining ingredients.

  • 3.Serve dusted with icing sugar.
