Menu Name
Nasi Goreng USA
Summary

Nasi Goreng USA actually has nothing to do with America, rather it refers to the dish's ingredients of udang, sotong, and ayam - shrimp, squid, and chicken
Ingredients
Ingredients Ingredients
  • Side Dish
  • 100g Chicken Breast (sliced)
  • 1/2 Brown Onion
  • 1/4 Red Capsicum
  • 1/4 Green Capsicum
  • 1/2 Tomato
  • 1 Garlic Pulp (minced)
  • 1 cm Ginger (sliced)
  • 2 Tbsp Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Tbsp Oyster Sauce
  • 1 Tbsp Chili Paste/Chili Boh
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • Rice
  • 1 Tbsp of Oil
  • 1 cup of Rice (cooked overnight, for 2 pax)
  • 1 Carrot (diced)
  • 1/2 Brown Onion (diced)
  • 1 Garlic
  • 1 Tbsp Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Tbsp Chili Paste/Chili Boh
  • 1 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • A dash of White Pepper
  • Garnishing
  • Cucumber and Tomato slices.

    		•
    Direction
    Method:
  • 1.Heat up pan and fry garlic and ginger till fragrant.
  • 2.Add chili paste and chicken. Stir fry until chicken is almost cooked.
  • 3.Add onions and capsicums. Continue stir frying until the onions and capsicums are fragrant.
  • 4.Then, add tomato sauce and oyster sauce to the pan. To have some gravy, add water to the pan and let the dish simmer at medium heat.
  • 5.Once gravy is simmering, add tomatoes.
  • 6.The dish is ready when the gravy thickens. Leave aside.
  • 7.Prepare fried rice by adding oil to wok.
  • 8.Stir fry garlic and onion until soft and fragrant.
  • 9.Add chili paste and then, carrot. Stir fry for about 2 minutes at high heat.
  • 10.After that, add cold rice and tomato sauce. Quickly mix the rice to ensure that it is evenly cooked.
  • 11.Then add soy sauce and a dash of white pepper.
  • 12.Give the dish a few more stirs and dish out with the side dish prepared earlier.
