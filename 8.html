Menu Name
Nasi Goreng Daging Merah
Summary
Nasi Goreng Daging Merah is a Malaysian dish.This literally means red-cooked meat in English. It is a casserole of meat pieces in tomato. Pieces of meat are first fried to a golden brown then slowly braised in a spicy tomato sauce. Peas are sometimes added to the dish, and it is garnished with shredded kaffir lime leaves as well as coriander. It is often paired with nasi tomato - rice cooked with tomato sauce or paste, milk, dried spices, and a sauteed rempah base of garlic, onions, ginger
Ingredients
Ingredients:
  • Boneless Beef
  • Red colourings
  • 2 onions
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 2 lemongrass
  • 2 tablespoon sambal olek
  • 1 cup beef stock
  • 3 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 4 tablespoon tomato sauce
  • 4 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon tamarind sauce
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 fresh red chillies (thinly sliced)
  • 1 large onion (cut to onion ring shaped)
  • 6 kaffir lime leaves (thinly sliced)

    • Direction
    Methods:
  • 1.Boil the beef in a pot together with 3 teaspoon red food colouring.
  • 2.Boiled until 3/4 cooked.
  • 3.Take out the beef but leave the beef stock to be used later.
  • 4.Blend the garlic, onion, ginger and lemon grass.
  • In a wok, heat the cooking oil.
  • 5.Sautee the blended ingredients until fragrant and bit crispy.
  • 6.Add in the Sambal Olek and stir well. Sautee until the oil separates from the sambal paste.
  • 8.Add in the beef stock. Stir well.
  • 9.Add in the chilli sauce, oyster sauce, tomato sauce together with salt to taste. Stir well.
  • 10.Add in the cut onion rings shaped, asam jawa or tamarind paste, cuts fresh chillies, stir well and leave them to cook a bit.
  • 11.When the sauce are about thicken, add in the beef and stir well. Leave it cooked about 3 minutes by using low heat.
  • 12.Taste the salt, dish out and serves with boiled brasmati or Thai rice.
