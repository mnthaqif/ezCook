Menu Name
Benjo (Egg Burger)
Summary

Benjo is a sandwich consisting of one or more cooked patties of fresh egg placed inside a sliced bun. Burger may be cooked in a variety of ways, including pan-frying, barbecuing, and flame-broiling. Burger are often served with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, cheese and condiments such as mustard, mayonnaise, ketchup, relish, and chiles.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • Pinch salt
  • Pinch pepper
  • 1 slice processed cheese
  • 1 hamburger bun, cut in half, warmed or toasted
  • Optional garnishes: Ketchup, relish, lettuce, sliced tomato, sliced sweet pepper, fried mushrooms

    • Direction

  • 1.Heat skillet over medium heat. Break egg into skillet. Break yolk with edge of spatula. Season with salt and pepper.

  • 2.When egg is almost set, turn over and top with cheese; cook briefly just to melt cheese.

  • 3.Place egg on bottom half of warmed or toasted hamburger bun. Garnish as desired. Cover with remaining half of bun.
