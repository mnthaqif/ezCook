Menu Name
Nasi Goreng Kampung
Summary
Nasi Goreng Kampung a type of fried rice which is kampung style fried rice very spicy and hot, traditionally flavored with pounded fried fish (normally mackerel), though recently fried anchovies are used in place of it.>
Ingredients
Ingredients:
  • 4 red chillies, sliced
  • 4 shallots, peeled and sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled and sliced
  • 1 Tbsp dried shrimp, soaked and drained
  • 3 Tbsp oil
  • 150g prawns, shelled and de-veined
  • 4 long beans, thinly sliced
  • 4 cups cooked rice
  • 5 stalks kangkong
  • Salt, to taste Suggested garnishes/accompaniment: Fried eggs, fried salted fish, fried fish crackers, cucumber slices, etc

    • Direction
    Method:
  • 1.Place chillies, shallots, garlic and dried shrimp in a small food processor and chop finely, adding a little water if necessary, to enable the blades to work.
  • 2.Heat oil in a wok and stir-fry blended chilli paste over low heat until well cooked and fragrant. Add prawns and cook for 2 to 3 minutes before adding long beans and Â½ teaspoon salt.
  • 3.After a minute or two, add cooked rice.
  • 4.Stir-fry for about 10 minutes until rice is heated through and chilli paste is well mixed.
  • 5.Finally, add kangkong shoots, stir-frying on high heat until wilted, about 3 minutes. Taste and adjust seasonings and dish out. Serve hot, with suggested accompaniments.
