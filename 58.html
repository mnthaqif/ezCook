Menu Name
Chicken Tempura Nugget
Summary
Tempura is a Japanese dish of seafood or vegetables that have been battered and deep fried. It can make mixture with chicken nugget
Ingredients
  • 3/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten

    		•
    Direction
  • 1.In bowl mix first 5 ingredients; add water and egg and then stir till smooth.Blend together

  • 2.Dip chicken into batter. Drop chunks (several at a time) into 1/2 inch of corn oil heated to 370 degrees.

  • 3.Fry until golden on all sides.

  • 4.Drain and serve
