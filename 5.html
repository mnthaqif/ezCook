Menu Name
Nasi Goreng Udang
Summary
Nasi Goreng Udang is a great dish which will sure impress your friends and family. Prawn fried rice delight is satisfying and simple
Ingredients
Ingredients:
  • Cooked rice – enough for 2-3 people
  • 8 raw peeled king prawns – cut into small pieces
  • 6 stalks of green beans – sliced into small pieces
  • 1 stalk spring onion – thinly sliced
  • 30g fish cake – cubed (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons dark soy sauce
  • ½ yellow bell pepper – cubed
  • ½ teaspoon chilli powder
  • ½ carrot – cubed
  • 3 eggs – scrambled
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 shallots
  • Salt

    		•
    Direction
    Method:
  • 1.Prepare all the ingredients: chop the vegetables, slice the spring onion and king prawns and pound the shallots and garlic or slice them very finely.
  • 2.Heat a wok on a medium heat. Pour in a tablespoon of oil. Once wok starts to smoke, break the eggs and stir to make scramble egg. Do not overcook the eggs. Set the scrambled eggs aside.
  • 3.In the same wok, heat 2 tablespoons of cooking oil. When the oil starts to heat, add the pounded garlic and shallots. Stir for few seconds until fragrant.
  • 4.Then add the chilli powder and stir well for few more seconds.
  • 5.Add the chopped vegetables and cubed fish cake. Let the vegetables cook for 1-2 minutes.
  • 6.Next, add the sliced prawn, spring onion and scrambled eggs. Sprinkle some salt. Stir well for 1 minute or until the prawn starts to turn pink.
  • 7.Add the cooked rice to the mixture in the wok. Stir well for few seconds.
  • 8.Add dark soy sauce to the fried rice and let it fry for another 1-2 minutes.
