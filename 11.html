Menu Name
Nasi Goreng Ikan Masin
Summary
This salted fish fried rice is same also like other fried rice . Only in the preparation surely salted fish become major flavour. Some using salted fish and some people using common salted fish .But usually use salted fish which have many flesh like mackerel or threadfin fish.
Ingredients
Ingredient:
  • 3 cup Cooked Rice
  • 5 Vietnam Chilies*
  • 3 Shallots* (* sliced)
  • 2" Salted Fish - cubed
  • Bean Sprouts
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Spring Onion

    • Direction
    Method:
  • 1. Heat up few tbsp oil & stir-fry chilies and shallots until golden. Dish out.
  • 2. Fry salted fish in the remaining oil until crispy.
  • Add in rice, bean sprout, fried shallots/chilies, salt & pepper.
  • Stir until well mixed.
  • 3. Serve with chopped spring onion.
