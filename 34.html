Menu Name
Breaded Fish n' Chips
Summary

Fish and chips is a hot dish of English origin, consisting of battered fish, and chips. It is a common take-away food.
Ingredients


Ingredients:

  • vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 500g (1 lb) red snapper fillets
  • 50g (2 oz) dried breadcrumbs
    • Direction


    Methods:

    Prep:5min › Cook:10min › Ready in:15min

  • 1.In a large heavy frying pan, heat oil to 190 C.

  • 2.Dip fillets into beaten egg and dredge in breadcrumbs.

  • 3.Gently slide fish into hot oil and fry until golden brown. Drain briefly on kitchen roll.

  • 4.Serve hot with chips and mushy peas.
