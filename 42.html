Menu Name
Durian Finger Crepe
Summary

Most durian lovers fall in love with this durian crepe recipe on the first bite. With a thin and light layer of crepe wrapped around soft custardy durian and fresh cream, what’s not to like! Every bite of this delicious durian crepe recipe gives off the rich aroma and bold flavour of durian, Malaysia’s national fruit. Put simply, it’s pure indulgence on a plate.
Ingredients


Ingredients:

  • 250g Hong Kong flour
  • 60g Sugar
  • 300g Whole egg
  • 60g egg yolk
  • 600g Milk
  • 15g Butter
  • 250g Rich cream
  • 500g Durian
    • Direction


    Method:

  • 1.Whip cream and chill

  • 2.De-seed durian and roughly mash with a chopstick. Chill

  • 3.Mix whole eggs and sugar with a hand whisk. Add flour and mix well

  • 4.Add in the remaining egg yolks and gradually pour in the milk

  • 5.Melt butter in the microwave and let cool

  • 6.Add melted butter into the batter

  • 7.Heat up a non-stick pan, pour a little batter into the pan and cook until done

  • 8.Wrap with durian filling and whipped cream

  • 9.Serve chilled
