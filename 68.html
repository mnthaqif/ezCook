Menu Name
Kuey Teow Hong Kong
Summary

Kuey Teow Hong Kong is the menu which is the noodle (kuey teow) is wrapped with the egg to make it crunchy. The gravy is cook separately and mixed various type of vegetable and it is very suitable menu for vegeterian.

Ingredients

  • 6 cloves Garlic
  • 2 inches Ginger
  • 1 packet kuey teow , or 1 packet Bee Hoon
  • 200 grams Squids , cut into rings
  • 1 small bundle Chye Sim , roughly cut
  • 2 tablespoons Cornflour , for thickening
  • Fried Onions , for garnishing
  • 1 medium Onion
  • 1 litre Chicken Stock
  • 200 grams Chicken Meat , preferably the breast, diced
  • 200 grams Prawns , shelled and deveined
  • 2 teaspoons White Pepper
  • Salt , to taste
  • 2 tablespoons Vegetable Oil

    • Direction

  • 1.Heat some oil in a pan. Add garlic, ginger and onions. Saut√© well and add chicken, squid and prawns till cooked.

  • 2.Pour in some chicken stock, mix well, add Chye Sim and pour the remainder stock in.

  • 3.Mix the cornflour with a bit of water and pour into the pot to thicken the gravy.

  • 4.Add kuey teow into pan and mix well. Add salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for about 5 minutes.

  • 5.Serve noodles in a bowl and sprinkle some fried onions on top.

  • 6.If using Bee Hoon, make sure to soak it with warm / hot water first until they become soft.

  • 7.Serve hot and garnish with fried crispy onions.
