Menu Name
Nasi Goreng Cina
Summary
Chinese Fried Rice is a Chinese food meal in Malaysia. The Chinese fried rice certainly popular among Malay and Chinese>
Ingredients
Ingredients
  • 1 recipe of cilantro rice (below)
  • 1 Tablespoon grapeseed oil (or any other high heat oil)
  • 4 large cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 1/2 onions. You can use yellow or red or a mix of both (2 cups/145g)
  • 1 heaping teaspoon ginger, peeled and grated (10g)
  • 1/4 head green cabbage, shredded (150g)
  • 4 cups mushrooms, sliced – any variety will do (260g)
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, sliced lengthwise and then cut in half (120g)
  • Cilantro for garnish
  • Sauce
  • 2 1/2 Tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 Tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp. white pepper
  • 5 turns fresh black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • Juice from 1/2 lime
  • 2 Tablespoons water
  • Cilantro Rice
  • 2 cups water (475 ml)
  • 1 cup brown rice, rinsed with cold water (180 g)
  • ¼ teaspoon salt (2 g)
  • 1 Tablespoon olive oil (15 ml)
  • 1/2 cup cilantro stems (25 g)

    		•
    Direction
    Methods:
  • 1.Put all ingredients in a medium size pot, in no particular order, turn heat to high and stir to combine.
  • 2.When water comes to a simmer (about 3-5 minutes), turn heat to low and cover the pot.
  • 3.Simmer for 24-30 minutes.
  • 4.If rice is still on the wet side after 30 minutes, turn up the heat and cook for about a minute, stirring frequently, allowing the water to be absorbed.
  • 5.Turn off heat.
  • 6.Stir Fry
  • 7.Heat grapeseed oil in a large skillet.
  • 8.Add onions, garlic and ginger. Cook for 6 minutes, stirring occasionally so that everything gets evenly browned.
  • 9.While onions, garlic and ginger is cooking, make the sauce by adding all of the sauce ingredients into a bowl and stir. Set aside.
  • .Add cabbage to the pan, stir and cook for 1 minute.
  • 11.Add mushrooms and 1 Tablespoon of the sauce and cook for 3 minutes, flipping with a spatula every minute.
  • 12.Add Rice and cook for 4 minutes, flipping occasionally.
  • 13.Add red peppers and sauce and cook for an additional 3 minutes (you want the peppers to have a slight crunch to them).
  • 14.Turn off heat and serve.
  • 15.Garnish with a few cilantro stems.
