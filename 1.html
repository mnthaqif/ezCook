Menu Name
Nasi Ayam
Summary
Malaysian Chicken Rice(Nasi Ayam Malaysia) is a delicious dish from the kampung(village). It comprises aromatic cooked rice, fried honey glazed chicken, zesty soup and a sauce with a zing
Ingredients
Ingredients:
  • For the boiled chicken: ½ chicken A clove of garlic, crushed Ginger, a two inch piece
  • For the marinade: Dark soy sauce, a tablespoon Oyster sauce, a tablespoon Ginger, a peeled one inch piece A clove of garlic, peeled and crushed
  • For the rice: Five cups of rice 3 spoons of butter 1 inch of grinded ginger 2 cloves of garlic boiled chicken broth pinch of salt
  • For the chicken sauce: handful of red chilli 2 cloves of garlic 1 inch of ginger 1 small spoo

    		•
    Direction
    Method:
  • 1. First prepare the chicken. Cut the half chicken into to smaller pieces and rub with the crushed garlic and ginger. Place in a large pan of water and bring to the boil. Put on a strong simmer and boil until the pieces are soft – about half an hour.
  • 2. Keep the water aside when you drain the chicken. Mix the chicken with the marinate ingredients – the soy and oyster sauce, the ginger and garlic. Cover and refrigerate for a couple of hours if possible, 15-20 mins at least before frying everything in a little oil on a medium heat until brown. This will probably take a good 25 minutes or so.
  • 3. On to the rice. Rinse in cold water and drain, then heat a little butter in a deep pan on the hob on a medium heat. Add the ginger and garlic and let them heat without browning for a minute or two. Add the rice, stir well, and add enough of the broth from the boiled chicken to cover the rice plus a little more. Add salt and cook according to rice instructions.
  • 4. Time for the sauce. Blend the chilli, garlic, ginger, vinegar and seasoning for this.
  • 5. Bring everything together. Place the cooked marinaded chicken on the rice and pour over the sauce.
