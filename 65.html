Menu Name
Chicken Burger
Summary
Some establishments serving chicken burger also serve chicken sandwiches, to give customers an alternative to beef. Such a sandwich may also be called chicken on a bun or chicken burger, and is served on a hamburger bun with similar condiments and toppings as found on hamburgers. While most chicken sandwiches in this context usually use fried or grilled chicken breasts, a chicken burger may also be made of a grilled patty of ground chicken.
Ingredients
  • 1 piece of chicken burger patty
  • 1/2 of small cucumber
  • 1 small tomato
  • salad dressing , any type
  • 2-4 pieces of iceberg lettuce
  • some butter or marjerine
  • 2 cloves of shallots
  • pepper and salt to taste

    		•
    Direction
  • 1.Wash iceberg lettuce and set aside. Cut tomato and cucumber into slices.

  • 2.Pat dry burger patty. Heat up wok with 1 tbsp of butter. Deep fry burger patty until both sides are cooked. Remove and use a paper towel to drain the excess oil. Cut shallots into strips. Fry until golden brown. Remove and set aside.

  • 3.Spread both sides of burger buns with butter. Toast them until crisp.

  • 4.Place 1-2 pieces of lettuce on one side of the bun. Next, place 2-3 slices of cucumber and tomatoes.

  • 5.Spread some salad dressing on top. Add in cooked burger patty and shallots on top of the patty. Place cucumber and tomatoes slices. Lastly, 1-2 pieces of lettuce. Add a dash of pepper and salt. Cover up with the other burger bun. Flatten the burger bun slightly.

  • 6.Place cucumber and tomatoes slices. Lastly, 1-2 pieces of lettuce. Add a dash of pepper and salt. Cover up with the other burger bun. Flatten the burger bun slightly.
